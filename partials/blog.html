March 6th, 2012

On Blogging.

When I was a much younger developer; I often wondered quite actively why the older developers around me were not embracing blogging. I felt that it was this excitig new technology that would change how we communicated. I wrote, and often felt that the seemingly wiser, older tech stars had much more to say than myself. So I stopped writing.

Flash-forward to Age 30 year, and I think of a quote:

'The silence depressed me. It wasn't the silence of silence. It was my own silence.'
-- Sylvia Plath, 'The Bell Jar'

More and more often I meet younger developers that have questions, with a yearn for truly satisfying knowledge. I may now speak.
