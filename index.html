On Blogging.

When I was a much younger developer, I wondered quite actively why the older developers around me were not embracing blogging (or at the time -- as it were called "online journals"). I wrote, and often felt that wiser, older tech stars had much more to say than myself. So I stopped writing.

Flash-forward to being 30 years old now, and I think of a quote:

"The silence depressed me. It wasn't the silence of silence. It was my own silence."
-- Sylvia Plath, "The Bell Jar"

More and more often I meet younger developers that have questions, with a yearn for truly satisfying knowledge. I may now speak.
